Course Description

MEC Professional is a career-focused intermediate program integrated with CA/CMA/CS, CUET, IPMAT and CLAT preparation. This unique program combines Mathematics, Economics, and Commerce subjects with professional accounting and finance certifications, providing students with a comprehensive foundation for their career.

Our program follows a 60% Practical and 40% Theory framework, ensuring students gain hands-on experience and real-world skills. The integrated approach allows students to pursue their intermediate education while simultaneously preparing for professional certifications (CA, CMA, CS) and competitive exams (CUET, IPMAT, CLAT) for entry into top universities and law/management programmes.

Key Features

MEC Professional offers a comprehensive learning experience designed to prepare students for both academic excellence and professional success, including CUET, IPMAT and CLAT.

  • 60% Practical and 40% Theory framework for hands-on learning
  • Integrated CA/CMA/CS, CUET, IPMAT & CLAT preparation
  • Industry-integrated curriculum with real-world applications
  • Career-focused approach with professional guidance
